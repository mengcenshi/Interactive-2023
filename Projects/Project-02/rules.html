My world of chess


Chess is a very special competitive game for me. When I was a kid, my mom heard that learning chess could exercise my mind so she took me to learn chess, but later I joined many tournaments because I found it really interesting. I feel that life is like playing chess, if you don't advance, you will fall back, only when you step forward, you can open up the situation and create opportunities; sometimes you have to learn to make trade-offs, to capture the big and put the small. Don't penny wise and pound foolish. Of course, the final goal is to "capture the king". Only to keep the goal clear and clear-headed to get the ultimate victory. And then at the end of the very confident to say "check", is also a super cool thing!
